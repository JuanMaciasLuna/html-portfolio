Best Movies According to Juanjo

My Top 3 Movies Ranking

Mulan

The best hero movie ever. The daughter of a famous warrior risks her life to save
his father from going to war again. She ends up saving China from the HUNO army

The Last Holiday

A sad and tired retail worker finds out she is going to die. She restructures her
whole view of life, travels and finds love in her attempt to enjoy the last days she had to live.

Tarzan

A baby is left orphan after his parents where attacked and killed by a leopard in the middle of the jungle.
He grows up with orangutans without knowing his routes until one day a beautiful human lady and him meet and fall in love in the jungle.
